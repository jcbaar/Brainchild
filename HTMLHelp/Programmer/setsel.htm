BCM_SETSELECTIONPOINTS

BCM_SETSELECTIONPOINTS
	wParam = 0;
	lParam = ( LPARAM )( LPCRECT )pPositions;

Setup the text selection as passed in the rectangle. The left and top fields of the RECT describe the selection starting point and the right and bottom fields the selection end point. The caret is placed at the end of the selection. Line and column numbers start at 1. When any of the given values is out of range the message returns zero and the selection is not set.

  • Returns zero on failure and non-zero on success.
pPositions
A pointer to a RECT structure describing the new selection positions. Line and column numbers start at 1 and can go upto the amount of lines in the text or the length of the line you put the selection marker on. Any values beyond this range will result in failure and the selection is not set.

NOTE: The column position is the view position. This means that with a tab size of 8 the columns covered by a tab are upto 8 column positions.
